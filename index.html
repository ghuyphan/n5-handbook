The particle は (wa) is a topic marker. It indicates what the sentence is about. For example, in 「わたしはがくせいです」(Watashi wa gakusei desu), "わたし" (I) is the topic of the sentence.

The particle も (mo) means "too" or "also". It is used to indicate that something that has been said about one item also applies to another. For example, 「わたしもがくせいです」(Watashi mo gakusei desu) means "I am also a student."
